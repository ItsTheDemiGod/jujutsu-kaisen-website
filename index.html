Cursed Energy

Negative emotions emitted by humans manifest as cursed energy — an invisible force that births supernatural beings known as curses. Only those with special sensitivity can perceive and wield it.

Cursed Spirits

Born from humanity's collective fear and hatred, cursed spirits haunt the living world. They range from minor nuisances to catastrophic Special Grade entities capable of destroying cities.

Jujutsu Sorcerers

Elite warriors trained by Tokyo and Kyoto Jujutsu High Schools, these sorcerers channel cursed energy into devastating techniques to exorcise curses — at great personal cost.

Techniques & Domain Expansions

Domain Expansion

Innate Technique

Domain Expansion

The pinnacle of jujutsu. Sorcerers materialize their innate domain — a pocket reality where their technique lands with guaranteed accuracy. Hit rate becomes absolute.

Used by: Gojo, Sukuna, Megumi, Mahito

Black Flash

Advanced Strike

Black Flash

A spatial distortion created when cursed energy is applied within 0.000001 seconds of impact. Amplifies damage by 2.5× the base power. Only the best can achieve it.

Used by: Yuji, Nanami, Todo, Nobara

Limitless (無下限)

Manipulates space itself at the atomic level. Blue creates attraction, Red creates repulsion. Combined as Hollow Purple — an erasure of everything between two points.

Ten Shadows Technique

Summons divine shikigami manifested through shadows using two hands. Each shikigami must be tamed in combat. The technique's pinnacle: the Eight-Handled Sword Divergent Sila Divine General Mahoraga.
